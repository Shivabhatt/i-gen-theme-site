This is the comprehensive UI/UX Design Blueprint for the **Solutions Page**. It translates the raw client content into a highly structured, modern, and visually engaging layout, strictly utilizing the established design system parameters.

---

### **GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM (Designer Quick-Reference)**
* **Container Max-Width:** `1200px` (Center-aligned).
* **Section Spacing:** `60px` vertical padding; `40px` horizontal padding.
* **Typography (Modern Sans-serif):** H1 (`48px`), H2 (`36px`), H3 (`24px`), Body (`16px`), Small (`14px`).
* **Color Palette:**
    * **Headers/Text:** Deep Navy (`#003366`) & Dark Gray (`#333333`).
    * **Primary Actions/Icons:** Primary Blue (`#0099CC`).
    * **Highlights/Secondary CTAs:** Orange/Red Accent (`#FF6633`).
    * **Backgrounds:** White (`#FFFFFF`) & Light Gray (`#F5F5F5`).

---

### **SECTION 1: Page Header & Overview (The Hero)**
**Design Strategy:** A clean, authoritative introduction that immediately highlights the scale of the offering (60+ algorithms).
* **Background:** Solid White (`#FFFFFF`) with a subtle, abstract geometric pattern in Light Sky Blue (`#E6F2FF`) to imply "technology."
* **Layout:** Center-aligned content block (max 800px width for readability).
* **Content Styling:**
    * **H1 (48px, Deep Navy):** "Our Solutions"
    * **Subtitle (24px, Primary Blue):** "Comprehensive AI-Powered Products and Services"
    * **Body (16px, Dark Gray):** Description of categories and how they work.
    * **Highlight Tag:** A pill-shaped badge displaying "**60+ Available Algorithms**" (Background: Light Sky Blue, Text: Primary Blue).
* **Interactive:** A full-width search/filter bar floating slightly below the text, overlapping the next section.

---

### **SECTION 2: Solution Categories (The "Big 5")**
**Design Strategy:** To prevent user fatigue from scrolling through five massive blocks of text, use an **Interactive Tabbed Interface**.
* **Background:** Light Gray (`#F5F5F5`).
* **Tab Navigation (Top):** 5 horizontal tabs (Urban | Financial | Education | Industrial | Retail). Active tab has a Primary Blue (`#0099CC`) bottom border and bold text.
* **Active Tab Content Area (White Card Layout):**
    * **Left Column (40%):** Large Placeholder Image (e.g., Skyline with AI overlay). Corner radius `8px`.
    * **Right Column (60%):**
        * **H3 (24px, Deep Navy):** E.g., "Urban Public Safety Intelligence"
        * **Body:** Smart governance, security...
        * **2-Column Grid for Features & Use Cases:**
            * *Column 1 (Key Features):* Bullet list with Primary Blue checkmark icons.
            * *Column 2 (Use Cases):* Bullet list with Light Gray arrow icons.
        * **Data Highlight Row (If applicable):** 3 horizontal metric blocks (e.g., `100+ Cities` | `300k+ Cameras` | `60+ Algorithms`). Deep Navy numbers, Small 14px labels.
        * **Action:** "Learn More" text link with a right arrow icon (Primary Blue, hover to Orange Accent).

---

### **SECTION 3: Product Family**
**Design Strategy:** Differentiate heavy server infrastructure from edge computing through layout variations.
* **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`).

**Subsection 3.1: Central Server Products**
* **H2 (36px, Deep Navy):** "Platform & Server Solutions"
* **Layout:** 3-column CSS Grid.
* **Card Design (Servers):**
    * Padding: `24px`. Border: `1px solid #E0E0E0`.
    * Image Placeholder (Top centered).
    * **H3 (20px):** Product Name (e.g., "All-Target Video Structuring Engine").
    * Body text description.
    * Link: "Specifications \>" at the bottom.

**Subsection 3.2: Edge Computing Devices**
* **H2 (36px, Deep Navy):** "Edge Computing Devices"
* **Layout:** 3-column layout highlighting the Small, Medium, and Large boxes.
* **Card Design (Edge):**
    * Background: Light Sky Blue (`#E6F2FF`) to visually separate them from servers.
    * Image Placeholder.
    * **H3 (24px):** Small Box / Medium Box / Large Box.
    * Badge: E.g., "16-Channel Input".
* **Shared Feature Footer:** A separate full-width row underneath the cards highlighting the 3 core features applicable to *all* edge devices using icons:
    1.  [Icon] **Link:** Hybrid access
    2.  [Icon] **Compute:** 50+ AI algorithms
    3.  [Icon] **Manage:** Full lifecycle

---

### **SECTION 4: Technical Architecture**
**Design Strategy:** Make complex data visually digestible without feeling like a boring textbook.
* **Background:** Deep Navy (`#003366`) – This dark section breaks the visual monotony of the page. Text is White.
* **Visual Element:** A vertical or horizontal layered SVG diagram.
    * **Layer 1 (Bottom):** Resource Layer (Cameras, IoT).
    * **Layer 2:** Compute Layer (Nvidia/Atlas hardware logos).
    * **Layer 3:** Platform Layer (Big Data).
    * **Layer 4 (Top):** Applications.
* **Interaction:** On hover, each layer glows slightly and displays the explanatory text in a tooltip or a dedicated side panel.

---

### **SECTION 5: Algorithm Marketplace**
**Design Strategy:** A high-density, easily scannable "catalog" view.
* **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`).
* **Header:** "Algorithm Marketplace" (H2) | Subheading: "6 Major Application Scenarios, 50+ AI Functions" (16px, Primary Blue).
* **Layout:** 3 major columns (Identity | Pedestrian | Vehicle).
* **Column Design:**
    * Top of column: A distinct icon representing the category.
    * **H3 (20px):** Category Name.
    * **Tags:** Instead of bullet points, display the specific algorithms (Face Capture, Clothing Type, etc.) as small, rounded tags/chips (Background: Light Gray, Text: Dark Gray). This looks much more modern than a standard list.

---

### **SECTION 6: Deployment Options**
**Design Strategy:** Clear, side-by-side comparison.
* **Background:** Light Gray (`#F5F5F5`).
* **Layout:** 3-column grid (Cloud | On-Premise | Hybrid).
* **Card Design:**
    * Clean white cards.
    * Large, minimalist icons at the top of each card.
    * Checkmark lists for "Features" and "Use Cases".

---

### **SECTION 7: Final Call to Action (CTA)**
**Design Strategy:** High contrast, pushing the user toward conversion.
* **Background:** Primary Blue (`#0099CC`).
* **Layout:** Centered text with a row of buttons.
* **Buttons:**
    * **Primary Action 1:** "Schedule Demo" (Solid Orange/Red Accent `#FF6633`, White Text).
    * **Primary Action 2:** "Get Technical Specs" (Solid White background, Deep Navy text).
    * **Secondary Action:** "Compare Solutions" (Transparent background, White border, White text).
